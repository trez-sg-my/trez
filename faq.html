FAQ

Everything you and your parents might want to know about EpicRush.

Is EpicRush safe for kids?

Absolutely! We focus on creating a non-violent, age-appropriate environment for children to play and explore.

Does it work on my tablet?

Yes! Our games are designed to work perfectly on tablets, phones, and computers using any modern browser.

Do I need to download anything?

Nope! All our games play right in your web browser. No installs or app store visits needed.

Are there any hidden costs?

Our games are free to play! We want to make sure everyone can enjoy the fun without worry.

How are high scores saved?

Your scores are saved right on your own device, so you can see your progress next time you visit.

Can parents contact you?

Yes! We welcome feedback from parents. You can reach out through our contact page or email us directly.

What are the game controls?

On a computer, use the Arrow keys or A/D to move. On a tablet or phone, just drag your finger to move!

Can I play with my friends?

While our current games are single-player focused, you can challenge your friends to beat your high score!

Does the game help kids learn?

Our games focus on developing hand-eye coordination, quick thinking, and problem-solving skills.

How often are new games added?

We are always working on new ideas and hope to add more fun adventures frequently!

Is my child's data private?

We take privacy seriously. We don't collect personal information from our young players.

What if I find a bug?

If something isn't working right, please let us know via the contact form so we can fix it for everyone!
